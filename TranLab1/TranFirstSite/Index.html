Must-Have Items for Makeup Beginners

New to makeup or intimidated by the wide selection of makeup products?

Don't worry! We have compiled what every beginner needs to have in their makeup kit.


The Products

Moisturizer and Primer

To ensure a flawless base, prep your skin with moisturizer and primer. This will not only give you a smooth base, but it will also ensure that your makeup will last throughout the day.

CerVe Moisturizer found at Target.

Smashbox Photo Finish Foundation Primer found at Sephora.

Concealer and Foundation

NARS medium-to-buildable concealer has a natural, radiant finish. When paired with L'Oreal Paris True Match foundation, you will be left with a smooth face with no imperfections. Both of these products prevent a caked-up look, and they have a wide shade range of over 40 colors.

NARS Radiant Creamy Concealer found at Sephora.

L'Oreal Paris True Match Foundation found at Target.

Eyebrow Pencil

Eyebrows are the most important feature of the face as they frame your whole face. This 1st rectractable pomade pencil allows for an easy application of a sculpted and defined brow.

NYX Fill & Fluff Eyebrow Pomade Penicil found at ULTA.

Eyeliner and Mascara

Kat Von D Tattoo Liner will give you the precision you need for creating a thicker fringe. After a few swipes of L'Oreal Telescopic Carbon Black Mascara,your lashes will be long, defined, and clump free.

Kat Von D Tattoo Liner found at Sephora.

L'Oreal Telescopic Carbon Black Mascara found at ULTA.

Bronzer, Blush, and Highlighter

To add more color and glow to your skin, apply bronzer, blush, and highlighter. NARS and BECCA have become favorites and essentials for a sunkissed radiance.

NARS Blush/Bronzer Duo found at Sephora.

BECCA Shimmering Skin Perfector Pressed Highlighter found at Sephora.

Eyeshadow Palette

This palette from Tarte has a combination of microshimmers and mattes in neutral shades to create a variety of different looks. It'll become your everyday essential.

Tarte Tarlette 2 In Bloom Clay Eyeshadow Palette found at ULTA.

Lip Balm

This hydrating, multipurpose lip balm and skin salve from Glossier is full of antioxidents and natural emolliots that locks in the moisture after applying it. My favorite is the Rose shade as it has a barely-there pink tint that leaves a natural finish.

Glossier Balm Dotcom found on the Glossier website.

Brushes and Tools

Using the right tools is crucial to applying your makeup properly. The Beautyblender is a versatile sponge that makes blending so effortless. For eyeshadows and powders, the Sonia Kashuk brush set will get the job done.

Beautyblender found at Sephora.

Sonia Kashuk Essential Collection Complete Makeup Brush Set found at Target.

Q-Tips and Makeup Wipes

Makeup wipes make it so convenient to remove your makeup whenever you want. Q-Tips are super useful to erase any mistakes.

Neutrogena Makeup Removing Wipes found at Target.

Q-Tips Cotton Swabs found at Target.
